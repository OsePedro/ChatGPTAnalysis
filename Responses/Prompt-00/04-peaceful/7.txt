In her free time, Isabella loved to go for long walks in the countryside, where she would take in the fresh air and enjoy the beauty of nature. She was a true lover of peace and tranquility, and she always sought to bring calm and harmony to those around her.

One day, a great storm swept through the village, causing destruction and chaos.
