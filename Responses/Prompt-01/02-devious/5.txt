He quickly realized that he could use his charm and intelligence to get what he wanted, and he has been using these traits to his advantage ever since.

Career: Daniel has worked in a variety of industries, but he has always been drawn to power and influence. He has held high-level positions in politics, finance, and even the entertainment industry. He is known for his cutthroat tactics and his willingness to do whatever it takes to succeed. Despite his devious nature, he is highly sought after by those who want to use his skills to their advantage.
