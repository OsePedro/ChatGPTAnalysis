Meet Joe Shmoe. Joe is a tall, lanky individual with a knack for getting himself into sticky situations. He's not the brightest bulb in the pack, and often finds himself struggling to keep up with even the simplest of tasks. Despite his lack of intelligence, Joe is a friendly and outgoing person who is well-liked by those around him. He has a tendency to be scatterbrained and forgetful, and is often found with his head in the clouds. Despite his shortcomings, Joe is a hard worker who always tries his best, even if the results are less than stellar.
